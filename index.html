UPDATES

0.3: Redesign

Released 09/12/13. Added a few new upgrades, such as the Molten Laser, the Pre-Charger, and a new job for the crew to perform, along with the new substance Stearite and some rebalancing of the game. A major change in the design and positioning, as you can now move around space, as well as a spaceship that has been added.

0.2: New Stuff

Released 11/11/13. Rebuilt some of the design stuff, added an asteroid, some other technical stuff. Also got some new features like upgrades to the surface sweepers, drones that give you ironide, and a system of hiring crew to recycle fuel cells.

0.1: Alpha

Released 4/11/13. First version, built just for fun. Features include Surface sweepers, Rock Breakers, and the basic mechanics, more to come soon!
